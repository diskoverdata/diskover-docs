### Overview
Diskover Data is a web-based platform that provides single-pane viewing of distributed digital assets. It provides point-in-time snapshot indexes of data fragmented across cloud and on-premise storage spread across an entire organization. Users can quickly and easily search across company files. Diskover is a data management application for your digital filing cabinet, providing powerful granular search capabilities, analytics, file-based workflow automation, and ultimately enables companies to scale their business and be more efficient at reducing their operating costs.Â
